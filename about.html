About Us

Welcome to Kids Oorja – Where Learning Begins with Joy! At Kids Oorja, we believe that every child is a spark of potential waiting to shine. As a trusted preschool and daycare, our mission is to create a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where young minds can explore, grow, and thrive.

Founded with a deep passion for early childhood development, Kids Oorja offers a balanced blend of structured learning, free play, and emotional support. Our age-appropriate curriculum is thoughtfully designed to foster curiosity, creativity, confidence, and essential life skills in a joyful and inclusive setting.

Whether it's a baby’s first steps or a preschooler’s first story, we celebrate every milestone with love and care. With trained educators, engaging activities, and a focus on holistic development, Kids Oorja is not just a school — it’s your child’s second home.

Join us in lighting up your child’s journey with knowledge, kindness, and the power of early learning.


Our Core Principles at KidsOorja!

Skills

To teach the children not just to study but to learn through play, inquiry and acquire 21st century skills.

Values

To make our children empathetic, strong willed yet grounded.

Attitude

To inculcate a positive and willing attitude among the Kids Oorja!

Vision

21st century skill development among pre-schoolers.

Globally Ready

To integrate Indian values with internationalism and a global vision.

Infrastructure

A state of the art infrastructure which makes the children want to come to school everyday! To have many skill-wise colourful learning areas rather than just classrooms!

We Believe

That all children are gifted. We just need to empower each one of them in the area they excel in. Kids Oorja believes in doing that!
