Programs Offered

At KidsOorja, we offer a range of thoughtfully designed programs that cater to the developmental needs of children at every early stage. Each program blends structured learning with play, creativity, and emotional growth.

Toddler Program (1.5 – 2.5 years)

A gentle introduction to the world beyond home. We focus on sensory play, social interaction, and basic motor skill development in a caring, comforting environment.

Nursery (2.5 – 3.5 years)

Our Nursery program helps children build early concepts through stories, music, movement, and hands-on activities, preparing them for structured learning in fun and engaging ways.

Junior KG (3.5 – 4.5 years)

Children begin early literacy, numeracy, and communication skills through interactive and theme-based learning. Emphasis is also placed on creativity and group participation.

Senior KG (4.5 – 5.5 years)

Prepares children for formal schooling by strengthening foundational concepts, critical thinking, and independence while nurturing emotional and social development.

Day Care (Up to 10 years)

In our daycare, we create a homely environment to complement mother’s affection. Our trained staff understands the importance of providing a safe, enriched and secured atmosphere to children for their development. The Life Skills Programme, GROW, helps children to be independent, self-reliant and learn the values, ethics which enhances their personality. Children are also engaged in guided play, rhymes, songs, story time and games, thus helping working parents to maintain a productive and stable work life.

After School Program (3 – 10 years)

After school programmes are a great way to explore, create interest, and nurture the hobbies. Research says that hobbies are a great source of bringing inmindfulness, focus, over all well-being, and relaxation. If hobbies are practiced from early years, children would grow up to be more balanced, calm, and stress free. Children have the option to choose what they wish to learn more than what is set by curriculum and stimulate their interest.
